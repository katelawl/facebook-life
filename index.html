The Day in the life of a Facebook Feed

Good Morning

While you were sleeping, there were 20 new posts about Donald Trump, 3 new videos about how to make delicious food, and 1 vague emotional post

Good Afternoon

Since you were at work, 4 friends posted pictures of their drunken adventures the night before, 2 friends posted inspirational quotes, and 1 person was tagged in an embarrassing photo.

Good Evening

Finally, you got home from work and your roommate took the cutest picture of you with a bunch of dogs. Now you can brag to your friends about how loveable you are, cause you like dogs.

Good Night

10pm. Time for bed. Why don't you just check Facebook before you go to sleep in case you missed anything "important" from the day.

... 1am. Now that you have sucessfully spent 3 hours looking through facebook, you can get a good 6 hours of sleep before work tomorrow. Thanks Facebook!
